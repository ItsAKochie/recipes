Soft Pretzels
- 630 g AP flour
- 1.5 cups warm H2O
- 0.5 stick melted butter
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 tsp salt
- 7 g instant yeast

Mix sugar, salt, and yeast in warm H2O.
Combine flour, butter, and yeast slurry in a stand mixer on low for 1 minute.
Knead on medium for 5 minutes until smooth.
Proof in bowl for 1 hour.
Portion dough into 8 equal pieces and shape into pretzels.
Bring 6 cups H2O and 2 tbsp baking soda to a boil and give each pretzel a 1 minute bath.
Bake at 450 for 15 minutes.
