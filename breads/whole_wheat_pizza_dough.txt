Whole Wheat Pizza Dough
- 1.6 kg whole wheat flour
- 1 kg warm H20
- 60 g honey
- 50 g oil
- 30 g salt
- 20 g instant yeast

Add the yeast, honey, and oil to the H20. Stir to combine.
Add 800 g of flour and combine.
Add the salt and remaining flour.
Mix until combined.
Proof for 1 hour.
Portion into 250 g dough balls.
