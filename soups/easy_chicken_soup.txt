
32 oz chicken broth
10.5 oz cream of chicken
2-3 cups chopped chicken leftovers
1 bag frozen corn
1 bag frozen carrots and peas
Poultry seasoning

1. Add broth, cream of chicken, chopped chicken and seasoning to a pot.
2. Bring to a boil.
3. Reduce to a simmer.
4. Add veggies.
5. Simmer for 10-15 minute
