Sweet Potato Tortilla Soup

- 6 cups broth
- 10 oz tomato puree
- 1 pound sweet potato
- 1 can corn
- 1 can beans
- 1 onion
- 0.5 cup cornmeal
- 2 tbps tomato paste
- 2 tbsp chili powder
- 3 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp cocoa powder

Sautee onion. 
Add seasoning and cornmeal. 
Add broth and tomato paste. 
Add veggies and simmer for 30 minutes.
Finish with lime juice.
