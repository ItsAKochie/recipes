Veggie Chili Mac

- 8 oz macaroni
- 2 cups broth
- 1 onion
- 1 red pepper
- 2 can of black beans
- 1 can corn
- 28 oz tomatoes
- 3.5 tbsp chili powder
- 3 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp vingar

Add chopped veggies, beans, broth and seasoning to slow cooker. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 4 hours. Add macaroni and cook on high for 15 minutes. Finish with sugar and vinegar.
