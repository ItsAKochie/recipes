Chicken Wild Rice Soup
- 4 carrots
- 3 ribs celery
- 3 chicken breasts
- 1 onion
- 8 cups chicken stock
- 3.33 cups H2O
- 1.67 cups wild rice
- 1 cup flour
- 0.75 cup butter
- 1 pint heavy cream
- 0.5 pint half & half
- 1 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp white pepper
- 0.5 tsp thyme
- 0.5 tsp sage

Bring rice and H2O to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer until tender.
Meanwhile, saut√© vegetables in butter over medium-low heat until soft.
Stir in flour to make a roux and cook on low for 5 minutes.
Gradually add chicken stock and seasoning, then increase heat to medium and bring to a boil.
Add cooked rice, chicken, cream, and half & half. Simmer for another 15 minutes.
